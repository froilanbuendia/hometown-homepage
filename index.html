Three Activities to do at Cerritos

Look around the library

The Cerritos Library is a great place to read a book or study. The library is also home to the Cerritos Sculpture Garden, which is a mini outdoor museum.

Browse around the mall

The Cerritos Mall is a great place to shop and eat. There are a variety of stores, restaurants, and a movie theater.

Go to the park

The Cerritos Regional Park is a great place to relax, enjoy the nature, and play sports. There are many playgrounds, sport facilities, courts, picnic areas, and a lake.

Fun Facts about Cerritos

  • Cerritos was called "Dairy Valley" and had over 400 dairy forms. The bus system is called COW (Cerritos On Wheels) as a nod to their history.
  • Cerritos transitioned away from being a dairy farm in 1956 due to rising land values and property taxes.
  • Cerritos Library was the first "Experience Libary" in the US.
  • Cerritos Sculpture Garden located outside the libary is like a mini outdoor museum .
  • Cerritos is the home of the Cerritos Center for the Performing Arts, which is a performing arts center that hosts a variety of events.
  • Cerritos is one of the most ethnically diverse cities in California.
  • Cerritos has a high concentration of boba shops per square mile as there seems to be a boba shop at every corner.
  • Cerritos was once the "Auto Square Capital of the World" as they state they are the world's largest auto mall.
